# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers, ML engineers, and technically confident local-AI practitioners who fine-tune and merge language models on their own hardware. They need one dependable surface for configuring jobs, choosing resource limits, monitoring execution, and driving the same workflows from an MCP-capable agent.

## Product Purpose

Sytra Studio makes local LLM fine-tuning and model merging understandable, reproducible, and hardware-aware. Success means users can configure and run supported workflows through either the desktop UI or MCP, understand where models and datasets are cached, control memory pressure, and confidently recover or inspect every run.

## Brand Personality

Expert, restrained, and dependable. The interface should feel like a precise engineering tool: direct without being cryptic, dense without becoming noisy, and confident without decorative excess.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboard styling, ornamental gradients, glass effects, oversized marketing typography, novelty controls, and decoration that competes with model configuration or telemetry. Do not hide important resource behavior behind unexplained automation.

## Design Principles

1. Make consequential settings explicit, especially storage, memory, compatibility, and output paths.
2. Preserve one mental model across the desktop UI and MCP tools.
3. Prefer clear defaults with visible overrides over hidden magic.
4. Keep expert workflows compact while explaining risky or unfamiliar choices in context.
5. Show validation, progress, errors, and recovery actions where decisions are made.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA for contrast, keyboard access, focus visibility, labels, and status communication. Respect reduced-motion preferences, never rely on color alone for state, and keep technical copy readable for users working for long periods in light or dark environments.
